Deployment Model

Where your data is processed — you decide.

Cloud — Choose Your AI Model

Process your documents using the world's leading AI models — Claude by Anthropic, GPT-4, or others based on your preference. Your files never leave your source system, processing happens securely in the cloud, and your data never trains any model.

Recommended

Hybrid

The best of both worlds. Everyday documents — emails, meeting notes, and reports — are processed via your chosen AI model for maximum accuracy. Sensitive materials — financials, NDAs, and board documents — are processed locally, never leaving your environment. Routing is automatic.

Local — Full Sovereignty

All processing runs entirely within your own infrastructure. No external API calls, no third-party models see your content, and no data ever leaves your environment. Built for organizations in financial services, legal, or healthcare — where sovereignty isn't a preference, it's a requirement.

Use Cases

The tools are here. The gap
is knowing how to make
them yours.

Generic AI tools give generic answers. The organisations winning with AI aren't using ChatGPT for everything — they've built private systems trained on their own data, tuned to their domain, integrated into their workflow. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Construction & Project Management

A mid-sized European construction firm managing 8 active sites had project data scattered across email threads, subcontractor WhatsApps, planning portals, and procurement systems. We deployed a RAG foundation that indexes every document, contract, and correspondence across all sites. On top of that, a forecasting layer now predicts material cost overruns 3–4 weeks before they occur, flags subcontractor delays based on communication patterns, and automatically generates weekly project health reports.

Deployed RAG Infrastructure, Cost Forecasting, Delay Detection, Automated Reporting
Outcome
Project overruns reduced by 40%. Site director briefing time cut from 3 hours to 20 minutes weekly.

Equine & Stable Management

A premium competition stable preparing 20+ horses across grand prix, young horse, and breeding programs had performance logs, training diaries, competition results, shoeing records, and veterinary histories spread across paper files, WhatsApp groups, and spreadsheets with no connection between them. We built a RAG system indexing every horse's complete history, instantly searchable by horse, competition record, training phase, or health status. A predictive performance layer analyzes training load, competition frequency, and recovery patterns to flag horses approaching peak form and those at risk of overtraining.

Deployed RAG Infrastructure, Performance Prediction, Training Load Monitoring, Team Mobile App
Outcome
Competition preparation time cut by 50%. Further development ongoing.

Legal — Boutique Law Firm

A boutique firm handling corporate transactions and employment law was losing billable hours to document retrieval and case preparation. We deployed a private RAG system that indexes every case file, precedent, contract, and correspondence — fully isolated, with zero data leaving their environment. A command center dashboard allows fee earners to search across all matters by meaning, assign documents to cases, and generate client briefings in minutes.

Deployed RAG Infrastructure, Matter Dashboard, Auto-Briefings
Outcome
Case preparation time cut by 60%. Associate onboarding from 3 months to 3 weeks.

Real Estate — Investment & Sales

A real estate investment firm operating across three European markets had property data, investor communications, market reports, and outreach histories distributed across inboxes, CRMs, and shared drives. We built a RAG foundation connecting every asset, deal, and relationship. On top of that, an intelligent outreach system identifies the right investor profile for each new listing based on historical transaction data, automatically drafts personalized outreach, and tracks responses — all from a single dashboard.

Deployed RAG Infrastructure, Investor Matching, Automated Outreach, Pipeline Dashboard
Outcome
Outreach response rate up 45%. Pipeline capacity doubled without additional hires.

Hospitality & Hotel Group

A boutique hotel group operating across five properties had guest records, supplier contracts, staff schedules, maintenance logs, and revenue reports spread across five systems with no connection between them. We built a RAG foundation unifying every property's data into a single private intelligence layer. A predictive layer now forecasts occupancy and revenue per property 6 weeks ahead, automatically flags supplier contract renewals, and generates weekly operational briefings for each property manager.

Deployed RAG Infrastructure, Revenue Forecasting, Supplier Management, Operations Dashboard, Mobile App
Outcome
Revenue forecast accuracy improved by 34%. Group director's oversight time cut by half.
