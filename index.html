About VecterAI

We are the generation
that builds what
AI promises.

VecterAI is an AI consulting and product studio founded by master's students at IE University Madrid. We don't believe in AI for the sake of it — we believe in precision-built systems that create tangible, measurable advantage for the businesses that deploy them.

Most companies know they should be doing something with AI. The challenge isn't access — it's direction, trust, and the capacity to build it right. That's where we come in.

As MTGA graduates at IE University, one of Europe's top business schools, we sit at the intersection of business acumen and technical depth. We don't outsource the hard parts. We build everything ourselves — architecture, pipelines, models, and interfaces.

Every engagement begins with a discovery session. We map how information flows through your organisation, identify where intelligence is being lost, and design a system that brings it back — permanently. Then we execute.

Our clients are mid-sized European businesses that want to compete with larger players. Companies that don't have a 20-person AI team internally, but want the same infrastructure. That is exactly what we build for them — privately, isolated, and tailored to their domain.

Deployment Model

Where your data is processed — you decide.

Cloud

Documents processed via frontier AI models. Maximum AI quality. Original files never leave your source system. Anthropic's terms prohibit training on API inputs — your data never trains any model.

Recommended

Hybrid

Operational documents — meeting notes, correspondence, reports — processed via cloud for maximum quality. Sensitive documents — NDAs, board materials, financials — processed locally. Routing is automatic.

Local

All AI processing runs on infrastructure within your own environment. Zero data leaves at any point. Satisfies the strictest regulatory and confidentiality requirements. Complete sovereignty.

Use Cases

The tools are here. The gap
is knowing how to make
them yours.

Generic AI tools give generic answers. The organisations winning with AI aren't using ChatGPT for everything — they've built private systems trained on their own data, tuned to their domain, integrated into their workflow. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Professional Services & Law

Firms managing hundreds of client files, contracts, and precedents lose hours daily searching through versions and folders. A RAG system indexes every document — searchable by meaning, not just file name. Associates brief themselves in minutes, not hours.

Outcome
87% faster document retrieval. New associates productive from day one.

Manufacturing & Flooring

Complex product catalogues, supplier price lists, installation specifications, and customer histories spread across email and folders. AI agents surface the right spec before a site visit, flag price changes automatically, and generate proposals from historical data.

Outcome
Quote generation time reduced by 70%. Zero missed supplier updates.

Private Asset Management

Investment memos, board packs, valuation reports, and LP updates distributed across inboxes and drives. AI indexes and connects every document, extracts financial figures and entity relationships, and delivers pre-meeting intelligence before every call.

Outcome
Portfolio monitoring automated. Partner briefing time from 2h to 12 minutes.

Real Estate Development

Projects spanning years generate thousands of emails, planning documents, contractor conversations, and legal correspondences. RAG makes every commitment traceable. Agents monitor project threads and surface blockers before they become delays.

Outcome
Commitment tracking automated. Project director briefed daily in under 3 minutes.

Healthcare & Equine / Veterinary

Medical histories, treatment protocols, supplier records, and regulatory documents need to be instantly accessible and linkable. AI classifies every record, surfaces relevant history before appointments, and maintains full audit trails automatically.

Outcome
Patient history surfaced in under 10 seconds. Regulatory compliance maintained passively.
